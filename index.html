Mission Statement

The name Exodus Crisis draws attention to the often overlooked immigrants in the US. The mission of this website is to inform people about immigration in our current day and debunk misconceptions. You must question political leaders and institutions you trust. We emphasize that immigrants don't have to be "exceptional" or "contribute to the society" in order to be treated humanely. This website is merely a first step to educating yourself. To make a difference, you must take action.
